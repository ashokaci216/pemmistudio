About Pemmi Studio

Pemmi Studio is a welcoming beauty destination for clients who value elegant nails, refined beauty treatments, and polished makeup artistry in a calm, premium setting.

A Personalized Beauty Experience

Every appointment at Pemmi Studio is shaped around your preferences, comfort, and occasion. Whether you are visiting for nail services, skin-focused beauty care, or makeup, we aim to make each session feel thoughtful and tailored.

Our approach combines a clean, feminine atmosphere with careful attention to detail so you can relax and enjoy a beauty experience that feels personal from start to finish.

What We Focus On

Our studio centers on three core beauty categories, each delivered with premium products and client-first care.

Nails

From elegant manicures to detailed nail art, we create polished finishes designed to suit your style and routine.

Beauty

Facials, skin care, grooming, and beauty maintenance services are delivered with a gentle, detail-oriented touch.

Makeup

Natural, occasion, and bridal-inspired makeup looks are designed to enhance your features with a clean and graceful finish.

Our Studio Values

Hygiene First

We believe cleanliness and proper care are essential to a relaxing salon visit and a professional result.

Quality Products

Premium products and careful application help us create beautiful results with comfort and consistency in mind.

Comfort & Atmosphere

A warm, welcoming studio environment allows clients to feel at ease whether they are here for everyday care or a special event.
